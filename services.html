Services

It is common for travelers to experience anxiety when traveling abroad to non-English speaking countries, particularly when exploring countries that have a different alphabet. As a professional and experienced travel advisor, we are here to save you time, reduce your stress, and offer you a hassle-free and curated travel experience in Japan.

BESPOKE TRAVEL DESIGN

We work through a consultative process to understand what you hope to experience through your travels in Japan and then design and book customized itineraries. This includes family travel, honeymoons/anniversaries, solo travel around Japan trips, adventure travel, wellness travel and more.

SURPRISE HOLIDAY

Surprise Holiday is the perfect way to make a special occasion even more memorable. Whether it's a marriage proposal, anniversary, honeymoon, birthday, or other special occasions, we can help you plan the perfect surprise holiday. We will work with you to create an itinerary that is tailored to your needs and preferences. From accommodation and transport to activities and entertainment, we can make your surprise holiday an experience that you'll never forget.

HOW WE WORK

01

TRIP PLANNING CONSULTATION

In this stage, we'll discuss your travel dates, interests, and preferences for your Japan trip. We will also outline the next steps and provide an overview of our professional fees, which vary based on the complexity and scope of the travel planning and booking services required.

02

TRIP PLANNING FEE

This non-refundable fee covers the bespoke design and planning of your trip. At this stage, we will leverage our travel expertise, knowledge, and connections to curate a personalized travel experience just for you. We will also work to secure exclusive benefits and perks that are typically unavailable when planning independently.

03

PERSONALIZED ITINERARY PROPOSAL

Once we’ve curated your travel plans, we will provide you with a detailed, bespoke itinerary that outlines all the key aspects of your trip. After reviewing your personalized itinerary, you’ll have the flexibility to request modifications. Please note that the itinerary includes up to three revisions. Any additional changes beyond these will incur a new travel design fee.

04

EXPERIENCE

Now, you will begin your journey around Japan with confidence, knowing everything is taken care of. Should you encounter any language barriers, don't worry—our Concierge Service is here to assist you. From the moment your trip begins until the end of your stay, you can reach out for support whenever you need it.

05

SHARE & TRIP FEEDBACK

We will appreciate it if you will share your experience with us. We also invite you to give us feedback.

LUXURY TRAVEL DESIGN AND TRIP PLANNING

Our travel design and trip planning fee starts at $150 per adult for up to 5 days of travel. This covers bespoke planning, including accommodations, dining, transportation, activities, and special requests.

The price may vary depending on the length and complexity of the trip. For a personalized quote, please contact us directly.

*Please note that travel design fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.
