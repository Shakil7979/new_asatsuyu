Privacy Policy

Asatsuyu Japan Luxury Travel is committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the principles and procedures governing the collection and utilization of information that you, our clients and prospective clients (hereinafter referred to as “you” and “your”) provide to us, whether provided in person, over the phone, via mail, email, or through our website www.asatsuyuluxurytravel.com (the "Website"). Any updates or changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on here. By accessing or using our Website, you consent to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy, and your continued use of the Website following any amendments indicates your acceptance of those changes.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your name, birthdate, contact details, including email address and phone number, postal address, and payment information when you make inquiries, bookings, or reservations with Asatsuyu Japan Luxury Travel.

  • Booking Details: Information related to your travel plans, including destination preferences, travel dates, accommodations, and special requests, may be collected to provide tailored travel services.

  • Website Usage Data: When you visit our Website, we may collect data such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site. This data helps us enhance your online experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • Travel Planning: We use your personal and travel-related information to plan and arrange your luxury travel experiences, including reservations, bookings, and personalized recommendations.

  • Communication: We may use your contact information to communicate with you regarding your travel plans, respond to inquiries, provide updates, and share relevant travel-related information.

  • Improvements: Information gathered through your interaction with our Website helps us enhance our services, website functionality, and user experience.

Data Security

We employ robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use industry-standard encryption protocols when transmitting sensitive data.

Third-Party Disclosure

Asatsuyu Japan Luxury Travel does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as necessary for fulfilling your travel arrangements or when required by law.

Your Choices

You have the right to review, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, or if you have any questions or concerns regarding your data, please contact us at info@asatsuyu-travel.com.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Website may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your online experience. You can configure your browser to disable cookies; however, this may affect your ability to use certain features of our Website.
