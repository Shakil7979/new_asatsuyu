About Us

Asatsuyu means "Morning Dew" in Japanese.

Asatsuyu 朝露｜Japan Luxury Travel is a boutique travel advisory specializes in curating unique and bespoke luxury travel experiences in Japan, no matter which season you choose to visit.

We provide our clients with professional expertise and in-depth knowledge about memorable experiences and amazing destinations in Japan.

We alleviate the language barrier, saving our clients valuable time and ensuring a seamless travel experience in Japan. With Asatsuyu Japan Luxury Travel, you can explore Japan with confidence, knowing that we have you covered.

Meet Our Founder

Clariss Ordoñez

With nearly a decade of experience in the tourism industry and luxury hotels, She has lived and worked across various regions of Japan after earning a degree in Travel & Tourism Management. She recognized that the language barrier can be a hindrance to fully enjoying travel in Japan. This inspired her to help travelers discover and immerse themselves in the country’s beauty with confidence.

"Being an expert in the tourism industry is overcoming the challenges of living in a non-English speaking country, how you managed and adapted the culture, traditions, customs, and etiquette. Asatsuyu means "Morning Dew" in Japanese. I encourage all our clients to wake up early in the morning and experience it firsthand. It's a beautiful sensation—one that connects us with nature and reminds us of life's simple joys."
